What is the problem anyways

Microsoft decided to stop Windows 7 updates for most of their Windows 7 computers and the 7th-gen CPUs that released after did not get Windows 7 support at all.

This website will show you the status of getting Windows 7 fully working on the Dell Inspiron 5566 and also a tutorial on how to do it with step-by-step instructions.
